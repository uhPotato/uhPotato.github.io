Who I am

I’m CC, a UX Developer working at Oracle in her 3rd year. I revel in dissecting complex problems and crafting elegant solutions that integrate seamlessly into a person's life. Solutions that make a positive impact on people's lives by having a holistic understanding of the way a person thinks and acts.


My Design Process


1. Strategize

Before diving into anything, I always outline the goals and break down the project into general steps. Then I plan out my process; taking into consideration time, resources, and scope. Designing solely for the end users is naïve and impractical. Design should understand and balance the needs of the business, the users, and the people building it.

2. Explore

Research. The people who will be using the product, the competition, other designers, tutorials, anything and everything. Also, at this stage I define my context & design problem; potentially creating design artifacts to help me empathize and understand the target users.

3. Ideate

Research and thoughts become mind maps & brainstorms. Ideas are scribbled down and sketches are small and drawn in rapid successions. Concepts are reviewed, evaluated, & tossed. A few rough gems of concepts are selected as the seed for further ideation until a final concept is chosen.

4. Execute

Choose the right tool for prototyping. It could be photoshop, code, or pen and paper; sketching out just enough to present the idea is the purpose for this stage.

5. Iterate

Design is never finished; it merely becomes good enough to solicit feedback. Sharing, critiques, and user testing provide feedback that is used to fuel ongoing design iterations.
